About

Stationæry is a fast, light and lean e-dictionary for the Web.
Accessible and print-friendly design.
Dark Mode available.
Words looked up are saved in history page.
Can be added to your home screen.

FAQ

Q: Why are some words unavailable?
A: Our source dictionaries don't have them. We can't do anything about that.

Q: How do I enable dark mode?
A: Enable it on your operating system or browser, and your setting will reflect on this website.

Stationæry is made by Gilbert and is open source. Word data is sourced from Wiktionary. Website URL: https://stationaery.vercel.app
